Host Livestream Concert for Students

4TH APRIL

8pm GMT

With the closure of venues and bars throughout the country and with the setting of deadline stress. The 1975 host a livestream, student only concert in order to help ease students who are currently trying to meet assessment deadlines during lockdown.

The 1975 will perform at the BlackBoard Stream Aid 2020 on Saturday the 4th April at 8PM GMT.
